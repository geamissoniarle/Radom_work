A little bit about the Htc U11 ...

The 5.5-inch screen puts this HTC at the top of its category. The resolution is also high: 2560x1440 pixel. The features offered by the HTC U11 are many and top of the line. Starting with the LTE 4G that allows data transfer and excellent navigation on the internet, plus Wi-Fi and GPS connectivity present in the handset. It also has multimedia player, videoconference, and bluetooth. We emphasize the excellent internal memory of 64 GB with the possibility of expansion. 12 megapixel camera. The thickness of only 7.9mm makes the HTC U11 one of the thinnest phones out there.
